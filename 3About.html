Charlie King

About Me

Photograper and Artist

Artist Bio

Charlie King was born in 2001. Growing up he was always expressed an interest in art and being creative. He chose to study photography at Sam Houston State university starting in 2019 and is planning to graduate in the spring of 2023 with a BFA in photography minoring in graphic design and art history. While studying photography in college Charlie has been drawn toward alternative processes, such as the cyanotype process, tintypes, and film processes in the dark room. Some themes that he focuses on include vulnerability, identity, and gender. His work showcases diversity in both gender, sexuality, and race, as well as the vulnerability that goes with showcasing the human form.

Artist Statement

My photography takes a close look at the human form and how each body and person is completely unique but interconnected at the same time. This work is a reflection of myself and my identity as well as those that I am photographing. I am a transgender and queer individual and my work reflects my identity, struggles, and joy that comes from my identity. My artwork explores connectiveness and individuality of people and the human condition.
